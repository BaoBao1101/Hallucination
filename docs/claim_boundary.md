# Claim boundary for the pre-human submission draft

## Claims supported by frozen results

1. In the Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite P1 matched corpus, M3 had higher
   LLM-evaluator-assessed technical plausibility than M1 under the individual
   protocol, with a Holm-significant positive paired effect for both judges.
2. Under the role-aware directional protocol, M3 retained a substantial portion
   of the reference creative core while receiving high ratings for practical MVP
   improvement and overclaim removal from both judges.
3. Under that directional protocol, both judges selected the transformation more
   often than the reference as the concept to prototype first.

## Claims not supported

- human preference or expert validation;
- market viability, user adoption, revenue, or startup success;
- factual validity of the venture claims in any card;
- universal superiority of M3 over M1;
- superiority of Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite over Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite;
- idea-level M3-over-M0 superiority;
- a neutral pairwise preference result.

All claims are limited to evaluator-assessed properties of the displayed
venture-concept cards in the evaluated corpus and protocols.
