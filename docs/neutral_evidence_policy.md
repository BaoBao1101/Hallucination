# Neutral-evidence policy for the pre-human submission draft

The pre-human manuscript does not use neutral paired outputs to support any primary
efficacy, superiority, equivalence, or prototype-preference conclusion.

Reason:
- Gemini neutral v1, v2, and v3 are excluded under the pre-specified
  score-to-preference consistency rule.
- The included ChatGPT neutral output is retained as an auditable artifact, but
  single-judge neutral evidence is not necessary for the manuscript's central
  mechanism claim and is therefore not used as confirmatory evidence.
- The paper's primary claims are restricted to the strict-valid two-judge
  individual and directional protocols.

This policy is conservative by design. It does not alter archived scores, audit
files, or exclusions; it only defines the scope of claims in the manuscript.
