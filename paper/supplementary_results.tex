\section*{Reading guide}
This supplement reports the full aggregated metric tables behind the main manuscript.
It excludes private mappings and candidate-level unblinding data. The Gemini 3.1 Flash
Lite P1 study is primary. Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite P0 and P1 are retained as supporting
developmental studies and are not pooled with the primary study. Negative M3--M1
deltas favor M3 only for risk metrics; for all other individual metrics, positive
deltas favor M3.
